Research Experience

Ballbot ,Beijing Jiaotong University(Mar.2016-present)
Research Assistant, Advisor: Prof. Chun Dong

  • Ported driver code(I2C, CAN bus) for Linux platform and embedded them in MATLAB/Simulink environment for the implementation of Model-based design.
  • Created Ballbot simulation model in Gazebo.
  • Analyzed the dynamics of the Ballbot and helped conduct trajectory stabilization experiments using LQR controller.
  • Designing learning model-based and model-free reinforcement algorithms for Ballbot.

Omnidirectional Mobile Robot ,National Engineering Laboratory for Video Technology, Peking University(Dec.2016-Apr.2017)
Research Assistant, Advisor: Prof. Yizhou Wang

  • Selected the mechanical configuration of omnidirectional mobile robot.
  • Designed the embedded system of it,including the PCB design and implementation of low-level control algorithms.
  • Optimized a robot communication protocol in ROS and designed an algorithm for avoiding obstacles.

High Power Density Motor Driver ,Academy of Opto-electronics, Chinese Academy of Sciences, CAS Student Research Training Program(Sep.2016-May.2017)
Research Assistant, Advisor: Prof. Jingguo Zhu, Feng Li

  • Designed total 3-phase full bridge motor driver’s PCB for BLDC and PMSM.
  • Experimental validation with control algorithms including FOC, SVPWM in Simulink and implemented them in Contex-M4 embedded system.
  • Ported the sensorless vector control algorithm for PMSM to improve its dynamic performance.
